About

I operate at the boundary between laboratory and studio. My practice is rooted in neurogeneticist — the study of how genetic code shapes neural architecture — and extends into visual art, installation, and computational design.

I believe the most compelling art emerges from genuine inquiry. Each project begins with a scientific question and ends with an aesthetic proposition. The gap between is where the work lives.
